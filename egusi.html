EGUSI SOUP

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of melon seed
  • 3 maggi cubes
  • 1 cup of palmoil
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 3 scotch bonnets
  • 1 red Onion
  • 400g tin plum tomatoes
  • 1 brown Onion
  • Assorted meat
  • Dry shawa
  • Smoked dry catfish
  • 1/4 cup grounded crayfish
  • Locust beans - Iru
  • Beef stock
  • 100g pumpkin leaves
  • Salt

Cooking Methods

  • Wash, season and boil the beef or chicken and assorted meats.
  • Blend the scotch bonnet, bell pepper, plum tomato and onion.
  • Blend the brown onion with the melon seeds to form a thick paste.
  • Clean the smoked dry fish by soaking in hot water for few minutes and rinsing well
  • In a pot, add palm oil, leave to heat for 2 minutes then add the blended pepper, maggi cubes and beef stock. Cover the pot and leave to cook for 10 minutes.
  • Add smoked fish, beef, assorted meat, locust beans and crayfish. Stir together and leave to cook for another 10 minutes.
  • After the 10 minutes, open the pot and stir the stew. Now add the dry fish, assorted meat and locust at this stage and let it cook for another 10 minutes
  • Scoop in the onion/egusi paste, cover the pot and leave to cook for yet another 10 minutes
  • Stir the soup and leave to cook for 5 minutes or till oil floats to the top.
  • At the end of the 5 minutes, stir and add vegetable to the soup.
  • Then add Kale or Ugu
  • Stir in the vegetable and leave to simmer for 5 minutes.
